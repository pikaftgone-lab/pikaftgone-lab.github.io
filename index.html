About Me

I am a compliance assurance professional and quality coach with over four years of experience in the 1st and 2nd Line of Defence. My work sits at the intersection of regulatory compliance, internal controls, and operational quality — ensuring that what is supposed to happen, actually happens, and that when it doesn't, it gets fixed properly.

I specialise in design and operational effectiveness testing, risk-based assessment, and coaching teams to raise their compliance capability. I have overseen up to 350 complex financial cases monthly and led structured remediation efforts that are now in implementation.

Beyond my domain expertise, I actively develop my technology skills — including data analytics, SQL, Power BI, and AI tools — because I believe the future of compliance and risk is inseparable from technology agility.

QC Audit Form Optimisation

Midland Credit Management · 2024–2026

Identified a design weakness in the QC audit form — 7 of 32 parameters lacked active business purpose, creating redundant data entry and inflating error risk. Conducted a six-month error analysis establishing that 85% of errors were data-entry-driven, with 72% attributable to the redundant fields.

Presented a structured findings report and remediation proposal to management. Now in implementation via PQM/IT collaboration, with go-live targeted May 2026.
