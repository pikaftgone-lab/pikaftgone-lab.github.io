About

Compliance professional.

I'm a compliance assurance professional who sits at the intersection of regulatory knowledge, operational quality, and people development. My work in the 2nd Line of Defence isn't just about identifying what went wrong — it's about understanding why, remediating the risk, and building the understanding in teams so it doesn't happen again.

I think in systems. When I see a compliance error, I don't just grade it — I trace the downstream consequence. A misclosed call disposition isn't a paperwork error; it's a live FDCPA exposure that needs active remediation before the system acts on wrong information.

Outside of compliance, I build things — Power BI dashboards and web applications. I believe the best assurance professionals are also technologists.
